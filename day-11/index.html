Day 11: Mine

07:30 Day started off with an early wake-up call, knee was aching because I left the gameready sleeve on it and it was propped up at an angle.
I put the brace on and slept until 11:30

Been trying to play this song 🎸 like Justin Bieber

Almost looking like my good side:


The injured leg is finally starting to feel like it's mine again. I am finally regaining ownership of that leg again.
It is finally moving, behaving like I want it to.

But honestly, are these bodies really ours to claim???
Or are they merely blessings, gifts, vessels that have been given to us...

Updates:

  • Walking with brace is 🍰
  • CPM cranked up to 90º because I can
  • Continually improving quad strength

🙌

  • Thankful for healthy eyes, hearing, breathing, smelling, and sense of touch
  • The way God puts people in your life just at the right time

🙏

  • Love my parents better, especially while I am at home
  • Allergy doctors respond to my cries of help
