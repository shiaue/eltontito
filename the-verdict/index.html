Day 6: The Verdict 😬

Prognosis:

No ACL tear (THANK THE LORD🙏 )
Bucket handle meniscus tear (requires surgery ASAP 🏃‍💨)

I decided to include a picture of the x-ray that they used on me because I wasn't allowed to bring my phone into the MRI room

Possible Outcomes:

Surgery 1:
Repair the meniscus
++ No increased risk of arthritis
-- Longer more extensive rehab (3+ months)

Surgery 2:
Shave most of meniscus off
++ Faster recovery (2-4 weeks)
-- Risk of long-time problems with knee (arthritis)

The Kicker:

I have no choice of the surgery option... (Oh the uncertainty)
Surgeon will try for Surgery 1, but if the repair doesn't hold, they will fall back to Surgery 2.

Potential Complications

  1. ACL is in fact torn (MRI usually doesn't lie... but the camera will reveal the FACTS)
  2. Infection post surgery
  3. Deep vein thrombosis

🙏 Prayer Requests:

  1. Staying locked in throughout another round of rehab.
    To be focused on my mission to serve God in Guateamala and learn something new each day
  2. This "suffering" is just a state of mind... just like weakness... and pain.
    Haven't we already been liberated from the prison that we lock ourselves in???
    Jesus doesn't have the key... HE IS THE 🔑
  3. Do NOT let physical pain give me a reason to treat others with less kindness, love, and patience. Everyone and their mother is dealing with some sort of pain. Complaining about my wound(s) does nothing to heal yours and vice versa.
  4. Swift and steady recovery (I really hope this is my last knee surgery... that is all)
